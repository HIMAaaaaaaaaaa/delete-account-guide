How to Delete Your Account on BILMALIM

If you want to permanently delete your account from BILMALIM, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your account in the app.
  2. Open the app drawer (☰ menu in the top-left corner).
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account".
  4. A confirmation pop-up will appear asking you to confirm or cancel.
  5. If you tap "Confirm", your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.
  6. If you tap "Cancel", no changes will be made, and your account will remain active.

⚠ Warning: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, you cannot recover your data.
